

# 🧭 1. Email & Collaboration Security (closest for inline M365 use case)

### Key players

* Microsoft Defender for Office 365
* Proofpoint
* Mimecast

## What they do well

* Inline email attachment scanning
* URL + phishing protection
* Native to email flow

## Where they fall short (your opportunity)

* **Email-centric only**
* Weak outside M365/email
* Limited developer/API-first model
* No unified decision plane across apps + storage

👉 They own:

> “email security”

You’re aiming for:

> **“file security everywhere”**
